The Shard Diffractor is a secondary weapon for the Engineer. It fires a continuous beam of superheated plastcrete shards, dealing direct damage and potentially setting targets ablaze. It consumes ammo quickly but offers sustained damage, making it effective against single targets and lines of enemies.
