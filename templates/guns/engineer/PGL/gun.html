The Deepcore 40mm PGL is a secondary weapon for the Engineer. This grenade launcher fires explosive rounds that detonate on impact, dealing significant area damage. It's highly effective against groups of enemies and can be modified for various roles, from wide area clearing to focused single-target destruction.
