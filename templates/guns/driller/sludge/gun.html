The Corrosive Sludge Pump is a primary weapon for the Driller. It fires blobs of corrosive goo that create puddles on surfaces, slowing enemies and dealing damage over time. It can also fire charged shots for larger puddles or direct impact damage. It excels at area denial and sustained damage through status effects.
