The Cryo Cannon is a primary weapon for the Driller. It sprays a stream of supercooled liquid that rapidly freezes enemies solid, stopping them in their tracks and making them vulnerable to increased direct damage. It's excellent for crowd control and neutralizing high-threat targets.
