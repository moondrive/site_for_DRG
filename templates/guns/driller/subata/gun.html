The Subata 120 is a secondary weapon for the Driller. It's a reliable semi-automatic pistol effective at medium range. It synergizes well with the Driller's primaries, dealing bonus damage to burning targets, making it ideal for finishing off enemies ignited by the CRSPR or caught in Persistent Plasma.
