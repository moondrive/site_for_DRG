The Colette Wave Cooker is a secondary weapon for the Driller. It emits a continuous microwave beam that cooks enemies from the inside out. It has unique interactions with temperature and status effects, potentially causing Temperature Shock on enemies affected by heat or cold, or triggering other effects based on active status ailments.
