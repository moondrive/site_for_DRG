The Experimental Plasma Charger (EPC) is a secondary weapon for the Driller. It fires bolts of plasma that can be tapped for quick shots or charged for a larger, area-damaging explosion. Charged shots can also be used to mine minerals from a distance ("EPC mining"). It offers versatility for both combat and utility.
